We Think...

We believe that going to different planets, such as Mars is good academically but do not think that it is realistic to have normal humans live there. Privatizing space exploration has its pros and cons. It will probably help speed up space exploration, however since the private organizations probably wont be non-profits, their goals might lean more on the making money side than the exploring space side. However, these different companies will create competition which might actually accelerate the rate at which we explore space.
